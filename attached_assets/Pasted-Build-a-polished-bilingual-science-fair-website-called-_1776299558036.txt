Logic:
Use a transparent rule-based environmental risk system.
- If temperature >= 35 C or apparent temperature >= 38 C, increase heat risk
- If humidity >= 80% together with high temperature, increase discomfort risk
- If precipitation probability >= 60% or rain is notable, increase rain risk
- If PM2.5 is high, increase air quality risk
- Combine these into overall Low / Moderate / High
- Show triggered rules clearly in the explanation section
